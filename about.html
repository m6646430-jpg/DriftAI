Our Story

Job hunting is broken. We're fixing it.

DriftAI helps job seekers — especially international students and skilled workers moving abroad — find real jobs, fix their resumes, and actually get hired. No ghost jobs, no fake reviews, no empty promises.

Why we started

Applying for jobs today is exhausting and often dishonest. Job boards are full of expired listings and "ghost jobs" that aren't real. Resume advice is generic. And for someone applying from another country — trying to understand ATS systems, visa sponsorship, and local resume norms all at once — it's overwhelming. We built DriftAI to make the whole process honest and simple: real jobs with real apply links, resumes written by real experts, and a team that actually replies.
