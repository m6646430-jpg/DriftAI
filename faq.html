Job Board
Is the job board really free?+

Yes — completely free, no account or payment needed. Browse and apply to as many jobs as you like.

Where do the jobs come from?+

We pull real, live postings directly from companies' own hiring systems (Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby and more). Every "Apply" button links straight to the actual job posting — no ghost jobs, no dead links, no reposts.

Which countries do you cover?+

USA 🇺🇸, Canada 🇨🇦, UK 🇬🇧, Europe 🇪🇺, India 🇮🇳, Australia 🇦🇺 and New Zealand 🇳🇿. Use the country filter on the job board to narrow down.

How often is the board updated?+

Twice a day. We also only show recent postings so you're not applying to roles that already closed.

Free AI Resume Score
How does the free resume score work?+

Upload your resume (PDF) and our AI grades it out of 100 across ATS-readiness, impact, keywords and formatting, then gives you specific tips to improve. It's free and needs no account.

Do you store my resume?+

No. Your resume is sent to our AI only to generate the score, then discarded. We do not keep it. See our Privacy Policy.

Resume Services
Are the resumes AI-generated?+

No — a real human expert writes your resume, ATS-optimized and tailored to your target role and country. Delivered within 48 hours, with a revision included.

What do the packages cost?+

Budget tiers (AI-built, expert-reviewed) start at $29.99 (Student) and $39.99 (Mid-Level). Hand-written tiers: Resume + Cover Letter is $79.99, Resume + Cover Letter + LinkedIn is $99.99, and the Complete Package is $149.99 USD. All prices in USD.

Job Hunt Program
What is the Job Hunt program?+

A done-for-you service ($150 USD/month). We tailor a resume and cover letter for each role and submit up to 30 applications a day on your behalf, plus a strategy call. You just show up to interviews.

Do you guarantee I'll get a job?+

No, and we won't pretend otherwise. Hiring decisions are made by employers. What we guarantee is that we prepare and submit strong, tailored applications on your behalf — consistently.

Payments
