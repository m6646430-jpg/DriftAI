Refund Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

We want you to be happy with DriftAI. This policy explains when refunds and cancellations apply. The job board and free resume-score tool are free — this policy covers paid services only.

Resume services (one-time)

  • Before we start writing, you can request a full refund.
  • Each package includes revisions — please use them if the first draft isn't right.
  • Because these are custom digital work products, once the final resume has been delivered and revisions used, the fee is generally non-refundable.

Community membership ($9.99/month)

  • Cancel anytime from your account or by emailing us — you keep access until the end of the paid period.
  • Monthly fees already charged are non-refundable, but you won't be billed again after cancelling.

Job Hunt program ($150/month)

  • Cancel anytime; you keep access until the end of the paid month.
  • If we have not yet submitted any applications for the current period, contact us and we'll make it right.
  • Because we begin working (tailoring and submitting applications) promptly, fees for a period during which work has been performed are non-refundable.

How to request

Email support@driftai.info with your name and order details. We respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method via Stripe.

⚖️ This is a general template. Refund rules can be affected by consumer-protection laws in your customers' countries — have this reviewed by a legal professional before operating commercially.
